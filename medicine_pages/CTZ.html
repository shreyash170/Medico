CTZ 10mg Tablet


Compostion:-


Cetirizine (10mg)


Uses:-


CTZ 10mg Tablet provides relief from symptoms such as blocked or runny nose, sneezing, and itchy or watery eyes. This will make it easier for you to go about your daily activities. It can also give relief from allergic reactions after insect bites and symptoms of hives and eczema such as rash, swelling, itching, and irritation.


Doses:-


Take this medicine in the dose and duration as advised by your doctor. Swallow it as a whole. Do not chew, crush or break it. CTZ 10mg Tablet may be taken with or without food, but it is better to take it at a fixed time.
